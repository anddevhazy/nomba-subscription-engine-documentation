<h2 id="h-security">What the login can't do</h2>
<p>Signing in gets a subscriber into their own subscription and nothing else, scoped strictly to the customer identity the phone or email resolves to. A few deliberate limits:</p>
<ul>
  <li>A subscriber can't change the email or phone number their account is registered under from inside the portal. That's an identity change, not a billing change, and it stays outside self-serve.</li>
  <li>If the one-time code doesn't arrive, the most common cause is a phone or email that doesn't match an existing customer record exactly, check the customer's record in your Dashboard before assuming it's a delivery problem.</li>
  <li>A portal session recognises one customer. There's no concept of a subscriber managing multiple merchants' subscriptions from a single login, each merchant relationship is its own identity.</li>
</ul>
