<h2 id="h-features">What the portal actually does</h2>
<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Capability</th>
    <th>Detail</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>View subscription</td>
    <td>
      Current plan, price, next billing date, and payment history (FR-13).
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Pause / resume</td>
    <td>
      Stop and restart billing without cancelling, without re-onboarding
      (FR-14).
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Cancel</td>
    <td>
      On by default; optionally captures a reason and offers Pause or Downgrade
      first (FR-14).
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Update payment method</td>
    <td>
      Re-tokenised through Nomba Checkout, the portal never sees a raw card
      number (FR-15).
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Invoice history</td>
    <td>
      Read-only list of past invoices, sourced from the same `GET /invoices` a
      merchant sees.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Deep links</td>
    <td>
      A scoped, signed, single-use link into exactly one of the above actions,
      generated by you or by our own recovery messages.
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
<p>
  Every action a subscriber takes here writes to the same audit log and event
  store as a merchant-initiated change, see{" "}
  <a href="/concepts/event-store">The event store</a>.
</p>

<h2 id="h-limits">What the portal doesn't do</h2>
<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Not supported</th>
    <th>Why</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Multi-currency, FX-aware billing</td>
    <td>
      Out of scope for this release; see <a href="/mission">The mission</a>.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Tax ID collection</td>
    <td>No tax-compliance workflow (VAT, WHT) exists in this release.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Editing your own registered email</td>
    <td>
      Security boundary. Changing the identity a login resolves to isn't a
      self-serve action; contact the merchant.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Seat or quantity-based plan changes</td>
    <td>
      Plans are priced per interval, not per seat. There's no quantity field to
      update.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Coupon-based retention offers</td>
    <td>
      No discount or coupon entity exists in the platform. The cancellation page
      offers Pause or Downgrade instead, see{" "}
      <a href="/merchants/customer-portal/cancellation-page">
        Add a cancellation page
      </a>
      .
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Custom branding or theming</td>
    <td>The portal is unbranded at v1, same look for every merchant.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
